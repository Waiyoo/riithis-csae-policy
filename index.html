Riithis Policy on Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Riithis strictly prohibits any content or behavior that promotes, distributes, or enables child sexual abuse or exploitation in any form.

Prohibited Behavior

  • Uploading, sharing, or commenting on content that depicts or promotes CSAE.
  • Soliciting minors or facilitating exploitation of any kind.

Prevention and Monitoring

We use moderation systems and user reporting to detect and remove harmful content. Accounts violating these policies are immediately suspended or banned.

Reporting

Users can report any suspicious or harmful content through our in-app reporting tools or by contacting support@riithis.com.

Legal Compliance

Riithis cooperates with law enforcement and child protection organizations in all cases of CSAE-related activity.
